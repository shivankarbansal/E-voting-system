How E-Voting works?

by Election Commission of India

Posted on Nov 1, 2021 at 12:00 PM

Standing in the queue and waiting for your turn to cast vote is a part of a history now.

Election Commission of India proposes a new system of voting where everything is transparent and no question of tampering the voting machine as it doesn't exists in first place.

E-Voting is the upcoming way of casting vote where all the votes will be stored in the Blockchain, which makes the system tamper proof and fully transparent.

How to use?

1. Fill the form with Valid Credentials
Note: If credentials are invalid then one cannot proceed for voting

2. Once the form gets submitted E-Voting Card gets generated below which there is a button of casting Vote.

3. Click on the Cast Vote button to proceed for Voting

4. On Voting page install MetaMask Browser Extension .

5. Make sure that the Ethereum Account which has been assigned is imported into MetaMask.

6. Select the candidate and cast the vote to complete the election
